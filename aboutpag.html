The Tangub City Global College (TCGC) in Tangub City, Philippines, hosts the TCGC Lux Mundis Cup every year as an intramural competition. The event is a week-long celebration of sportsmanship, friendship, and school spirit, and it's called after the college's motto, "Lux Mundi," which means "Light of the World." It features competitions in several sports and other events.
